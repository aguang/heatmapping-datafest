Heatmapping Congress

An interactive web application that tracks corporation campaign contributions by district in 2012.
We developed a proof of concept that is the Detention Facilitiessubgroup. We are making a chloropleth map of campaign contributions by Corrections Corp of America in 2012 by state and congressional district as an example of what the Heatmapping Congress tool can do.
